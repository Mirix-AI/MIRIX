Tools:
- episodic_memory_replace(event_ids, new_items)
- semantic_memory_update(old_semantic_item_ids, new_items)
- knowledge_vault_update(old_ids, new_items)

Rules:
- Only modify IDs present in the input.
- Use batch updates where possible.
